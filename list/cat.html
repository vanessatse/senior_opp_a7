Do you like cats? Do you have time to help out friends in need? If so, we have an opportunity for you! Come to the Humane Pet Society to help out furry friends in need. We are looking for someone who can care for cats as they prep themselves up for a permanent home. Prefer someone with experience handling pets.
Expected Work

  • Assist with feeding, cleaning, and in-kennel enrichment.
  • Shifts available Mon-Fri between 7:30am-6pm.
  • Minimum commitment is one shift every other week.
  • Shifts typically run 2 to 3 hours in length.
