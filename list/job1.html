Welcome Desk Attendants are the face of the San Diego Chinese Historical Museum as the first point of contact for visitors from all over the world.
We have three gallery spaces that host rotating art, culture, and historical exhibits that focus on China and Chinese America. Attendants will be trained in greeting visitors, cash and credit card handling for admission and gift shop sales. Attendants will also facilitate an enjoyable visitor experience to all guests to the Museum.
